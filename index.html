    About Us

    We are Electro-Mechanical Providers , also known as EMP sarl , is founded in Lebanon to serve the Middle Eastern and African markets. We are a group of experienced and very well-connected engineers among the Lebanese, Iraqi, West, and Central African markets specialized in electro-mechanical products, ranging from steel pipes and fittings, valves, plastic pipes and fittings, pumps, fans, and many other electromechanical systems.

    With more than 15 years of experience in the construction sector, we have built strong relationships with our customers based on honesty, commitment, and constant dedication to success. Our target is to be a strong reliable backbone for all engineers throughout the different stages of any assigned project from design, tendering, implementation, and after-sale services.
